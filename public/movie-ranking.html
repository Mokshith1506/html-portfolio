The Best Movies and series

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village.

Your Name

The film is about two teenagers who share a profound, magical connection after discovering they are swapping bodies. Things become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.
